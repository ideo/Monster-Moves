Hey Kids!
 The Monster Moves gang is ready to groove! Help them boogie down by choosing what moves will go into each monster’s dance routine. Will they twirl, flip, sway or swing? You choose! You’re the choreographer who can make it all happen!

Tap the dance tiles to sequence the moves and then watch each monster strut his or her stuff. Each monster has a full repertoire of unique dance moves and accompanying catchy musical beats that will inspire you to get up and dance along. Coordinate up to three monsters dancing at a time. This is one rocking monster dance party!
